Hi, my name is Katie and I have some advice for you!

Try, Try, Again!

If things seem easy at first but then become confusing once you're trying to do things on your own, don't worry you are not alone! Doing something step-by-step is easy nut when doing things on your own, repitition is the key.

I'm not afraid to admit that I did my first project three times before I moved on to the next lesson. Even if at first you do succeed, still try try and try it again. By the third time around I wasn't even using any of my notes and I created a beautiful website and went on to the bonus lesson to create a second webpage to my site.

You are human

Don't be afraid to take a break. Whether that means a day or 5 days. We are all humans here, not robots. Let the knowledge sink into your brain before you jump into another new thing. Rushing things is never a good way to learn. That's a huge reason why self-paced programs are so awesome! You don't have to push yourself to learn everything all at one time. The important thing is that you know and understand what you are doing before moving onto something new.
