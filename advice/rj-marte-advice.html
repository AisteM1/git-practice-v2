Hi, my name is RJ and I have some advice for you!

Accept that things being new and scary is fine!

Any time you start something new there comes a potential mountain of material that will seem completely foreign and, potentially, overwhelming. And that's totally fine!

Focus on each individual step rather than the full journey

Like I said in the previous section, learning something new can feel overwhelming. The best way to overcome that feeling is to focus on the step you're currently at, rather than the whole of the journey itself.

This way, instead of that same mountain, you're only focusing on each crack or rock you're stepping over. A much easier challenge to overcome, wouldn't you say?
